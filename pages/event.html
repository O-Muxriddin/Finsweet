HOW TO TRULY TRUST SOMEONE

Trusting God's Word: We live in a world where trust is often broken. This Sunday, we will explore what it means to place our absolute trust in the unwavering promises found within the Scriptures. We will look at biblical examples of those who found strength and security by relying solely on God.

The Lectionary Reading: We will focus on key verses from the Book of Psalms, exploring how faith in the Divine is the ultimate cornerstone for establishing genuine trust in human relationships.

Key Focus: Discover the path to authentic and lasting trust by understanding the true nature of God's faithfulness and how it transforms our capacity to trust others in our community.
