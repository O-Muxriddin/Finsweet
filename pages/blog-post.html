A PLACE OF WORSHIP AND COMMUNITY

We extend a warm welcome to all who join us. Our mission is to share the light of faith, foster spiritual growth, and serve our neighbors with love and compassion. Here, you will find a supportive family dedicated to living out the teachings of the Bible every day.

The core of our fellowship is built upon scripture, community, and outreach. We commit to uplifting one another, engaging in deep study of the Word, and providing aid to those in need both locally and globally. Everyone is welcome to explore their faith journey here.
