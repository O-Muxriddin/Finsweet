CHURCH WAS DOING WHAT HE OFTEN
DID WHEN DROPPED AN ORACLE

This phrase shows that receiving an oracle was not something strange or surprising for them. The church already had experience with prophetic messages, so they followed their usual steps, traditions, and reactions. In other words, nothing unusual happened — everything went the same way it always went when a spiritual message came.
